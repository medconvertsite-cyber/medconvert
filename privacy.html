Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to MedConvert ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit medconvert.io (or our equivalent domain). By using our website, you agree to the practices described here.

⚕️ MedConvert is an educational tool for medical students and healthcare professionals. We do not collect any patient data. No information entered into our converters is stored or transmitted.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the site:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic region — collected automatically via analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device to remember preferences and enable advertising. See Section 4 for more.
  • No personal health data: Values entered into our medical calculators (weights, doses, lab values) are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve our tools
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense and similar networks
  • To ensure the technical operation and security of the website

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense and may use other third-party advertising networks to display ads on our site. These networks may use cookies and web beacons to show ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

We may also work with pharma and healthcare advertising partners. These partners are required to comply with applicable advertising standards and do not receive any personally identifiable information from us.

4. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behaviour (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense and partner networks to serve relevant ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out of interest-based advertising via AboutAds.info or NAI opt-out.

5. Third-Party Services

Our site may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdSense
  • Google Fonts (font delivery)

6. Children's Privacy

Our site is intended for medical students and healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. As noted above, no health-related data entered into our tools is transmitted or stored anywhere outside your own browser.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Since we hold minimal personal data, most requests can be fulfilled by clearing your browser cookies. For other requests, contact us below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
