🩺 Med Student Tips

💡 To convert mg/dL glucose → mmol/L: divide by 18
💡 Normal body temp: 36.5–37.5°C (97.7–99.5°F)
💡 1 kg ≈ 2.2 lbs. Always use kg for drug dosing.
💡 Maintenance fluid rate (Holliday-Segar): 4 mL/kg/hr for first 10 kg
💡 GFR < 60 mL/min/1.73m² for > 3 months = CKD
