About This Site

MedConvert was created by a medical student who was tired of Googling conversion factors mid-ward round. The goal was simple: one clean, mobile-friendly page with all the conversions you actually need during clinical training.

All calculations run entirely in your browser — nothing you type is ever sent to a server. The site is free to use and always will be. It's supported by advertising, which helps cover hosting and development costs.

We update our reference values based on the latest guidelines from WHO, BNF, and major clinical pharmacology references.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer: MedConvert is intended for educational purposes only. All calculators and reference values are provided as a convenience tool and should never replace clinical judgment, institutional protocols, or verified pharmacological references. Always cross-check critical calculations with your hospital pharmacy or a qualified prescriber before administration to patients.
